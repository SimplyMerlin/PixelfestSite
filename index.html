Pixelfest.

A music festival in everyone's favorite blockgame.


What is pixelfest?

Pixelfest is a music festival in Minecraft. Here you can watch your favorite artists perform by simply joining the minecraft server when the event is being held.

How do I attend?

Simply join the ip play.pixelfest.live on Minecraft version 1.16.5 when the event is being held.

How do I perform?

Just shoot CrystalSlime or Merlin a message in the Pixelfest discord.

When are you holding the event?

Sometime soon in 2021. We are currently still figuring out what the scale of the event is gonna be, and we're still looking for new artists to perform.

I want to support the event, what do I do?

Share the event with your friends! Just shoot the discord or this webpage to some friends of yours. Ofcourse donations are always welcome, feel free to donate on https://store.pixelfest.live/. We actually really need the money right now, if we get just a few more donations we are able to massively upgrade the servers.

I want to help with the event!

If you are able to build well, feel free to message either CrystalSlime or Merlin with some screenshots of some builds of yours. If you can code in java, we might just need your help too, message me about that.
